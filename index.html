About Us

Welcome to Recykool - your smart e-waste solution!

We're on a mission to revolutionize how e-waste is handled, starting right from the grassroots. Our focus? Nearby neighborhoods and gali-mahaullas.

  • Committed to creating a better environment, one electronic device at a time
  • User-friendly web-app lets you easily book pickups or find the nearest collection centres
  • For every contribution you make, we offer redeemable points as a token of appreciation.

Join us in our journey towards a cleaner, greener future. Together, let's make a difference with Recykool!
